## Meet Mailbox for Workflows{{ date: '2025-08-22T00:00Z' }}

<h3 className="text-sm font-medium text-gray-500 dark:text-gray-400">
  Never lose email context again - track every message tied to your workflows
</h3>

<div className="mb-6 flex gap-2">
  <Badge type="app" />
</div>

You told us outgoing and incoming emails in workflows were hard to track, context vanished once processing finished. So we built Mailbox: a clear, organized home for every email your workflows touch.

<div className="inline-flex items-center gap-x-2">
  <SparkleIcon className="h-5 w-5 text-sky-400" />
  <h3 className="text-lg font-semibold">Why it matters</h3>
</div>

- Keep the full thread and context for every processed email.
- See incoming and outgoing messages side‑by‑side.
- Search past runs; filter by workflow, subject, or sender.

<div className="mt-6 inline-flex items-center gap-x-2">
  <SparkleIcon className="h-5 w-5 text-sky-400" />
  <p className="text-lg font-semibold">How it works</p>
</div>

- Each workflow run creates or updates an email thread.
- Messages, metadata, and attachments are stored and linked from run logs.
- Jump from an execution to its email thread in one click.

<div className="mt-6 inline-flex items-center gap-x-2">
  <SparkleIcon className="h-5 w-5 text-sky-400" />
  <p className="text-lg font-semibold">Built for teams</p>
</div>

- Shared view across your workspace with role‑based access.
- Privacy‑first: only your org's mail is stored; nothing is shared.
